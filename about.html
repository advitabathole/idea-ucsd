Mission

The IDEA Engineering Student Center supports undergraduate and graduate engineering students through academic enrichment, community engagement, and professional development programs.

The page is organized to match the clean UCSD department-style layout: clear header, left navigation, concise mission copy, and staff cards.

Values

Access

Helping students find resources, opportunities, and support without friction.

Community

Creating spaces where engineering students build belonging and connection.

Success

Supporting academic, professional, and personal growth.
