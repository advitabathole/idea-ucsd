About IDEA

Meet the team supporting Jacobs School students.

The IDEA Engineering Student Center advances student success through community, access, academic support, professional development, and belonging.

Mission Values Meet the Staff Contact

Mission

The IDEA Engineering Student Center supports engineering students at UC San Diego by connecting them with resources, opportunities, and a student-centered community that helps them thrive.

This page is structured as a cleaner staff and mission page for review, with consistent navigation, stronger layout hierarchy, and clearer information architecture.

Values

Access

Reducing barriers so students can find support, programs, and opportunities.

Community

Building spaces where engineering students feel seen, connected, and supported.

Success

Helping students grow academically, professionally, and personally.
