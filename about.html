Mission & Vision

About the IDEA Engineering Student Center

The IDEA Engineering Student Center supports undergraduate and graduate engineering students through academic enrichment, community engagement, and professional development programs. We aim to promote student success, belonging, and achievement across all engineering disciplines at UC San Diego.

IDEA stands for Inclusion, Diversity, Excellence & Arts — the core values that guide every program, event, and interaction at our center.

"We believe every student who comes through the Jacobs School of Engineering deserves the resources, community, and support to thrive — not just survive."

Our programs serve students at every stage of their academic journey: from first-generation college students navigating their first year, to doctoral candidates preparing to defend their dissertations. We meet students where they are.

Our Foundation

The Four Pillars of IDEA

Inclusion

Creating welcoming spaces and programs that affirm the identities and experiences of all engineering students, especially those from historically underrepresented communities.

Diversity

Celebrating the range of backgrounds, perspectives, and identities that make the Jacobs School of Engineering stronger — and actively working to remove barriers to participation.

Excellence

Providing the academic support, mentorship, and professional development resources students need to achieve at the highest levels in their engineering careers.

Arts

Honoring the creative dimension of engineering — and the whole person behind the student — by celebrating artistic expression, interdisciplinary thinking, and cultural richness.
